StructuralPointConnection

Node

The basic object that specifies the geometry of the analytical model and its members. The nodes define other entity types. Each node is defined by position in modelling space and by its name. For example, a 1D member (StructuralCurveMember) is defined primarily by its two end-points that are nothing else but two nodes.

If required, special boundary conditions can be defined for the StructuralPointConnection by StructuralPointSupport.
