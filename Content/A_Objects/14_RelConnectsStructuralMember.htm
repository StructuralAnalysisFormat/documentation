RelConnectsStructuralMember

Hinges on 1D member

The entity RelConnectsStructuralMember defines all needed properties describing the connection between structural members (beams, columns) and structural connection objects (nodes or supports). The connection may be rigid or free or anything in between. In each direction (translations along X, Y, and Z local 1D member axes, rotation around X, Y, and Z local 1D member axes) the condition may be: Free, Rigid, Flexible.
