StructuralRigidLink

Rigid Link

A virtual connection of two nodes from(StructuralPointConnection) or (StructuralCurveMember) for internal nodes on 1D members, where structural behavior could be defined. Master and slave node have to be defined. The rigid links are used for the connection, where you want to simulate infinite rigidity or user defined properties.
