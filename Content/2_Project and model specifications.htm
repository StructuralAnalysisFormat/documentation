Project and model specifications

Keep in mind: Structure of sheets "Project" and "Model" is different from all the other sheets. Properties are oriented in columns not in rows as usual. See the excel files below.

- each group consist of multiple objects, ignoring the group is taken in to account as ignoring all objects in the group (groups are parent to objects)
