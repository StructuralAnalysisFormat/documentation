Formcodes

The shape of the cross-section is uniquely identified by a so called Formcode. The Formcode defines the shape, the parameters which describe the shape and in some cases also additional parameters like distance between bolt holes, unit warping ordinates etc.

 

Note:

- "Profile orientation in library" defines formcode of manufactured cross-section. Together with the attribute "LCS of cross-section" from the sheet Project and model specification are defining the exact orientation of cross-section in the model. This is important especially for the cross-section asymmetrical around vertical axis.
