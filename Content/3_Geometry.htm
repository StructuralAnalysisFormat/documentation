Geometry

Basic construction elements

Basic construction elements are simple geometry types, that are used for shape definition of structural members. With defining the further attributes to these elements from the lists Structural analysis elements, Supports and hinges and Loads the complete structural model is created.

 

Following geometrical types are available for structural members.
